Makker Safe Power Systems AS

As Norway's leading provider of emergency power systems, Makker utilizes INNOMEGA's advanced web configuration system in the backend for effective quotation management.

HENSEL Fahrzeugbau GmbH

HENSEL specializes in building customized vehicles for emergency services, fire defense, THW, as well as tailor-made vehicles for craftsmen. Many of these can be configured online with our user-friendly web configurator. Additionally, HENSEL produces some of the market's leading industrial trolleys, ideal for the transport of operational materials for emergency services, hospitals, and other critical areas of use. In Sweden, HENSEL's trolleys are available through INNOMEGA. Contact us for more information.

Tomoku Hus AB

Tomoku Hus manufactures house components for Sweden House in Japan. In Sweden, they offer windows, as well as elegant Attefall houses and larger house models in classic Dalarna style. INNOMEGA provides maintenance of their website, and continuously carry out photography and video productions. Additionally, we handle layout for brochures and advertisements, all to highlight the quality and style of Tomoku Hus products.

ZKF e.V.

The Central Organisation for Bodywork and Vehicle Technology (ZKF), which is the industry association for over 3200 companies in the bodywork and vehicle manufacturing sector, has its headquarters in Friedberg/Hessen. INNOMEGA offers a cost-effective and comprehensive web and service package to ZKF's members through the project ZKFonline, which is tailored to meet the industry's needs.
